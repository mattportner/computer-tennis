# Compton Pledge
[comptonpledge.org](https://comptonpledge.org/)

<br></br>

The Compton Pledge is a groundbreaking guaranteed income pilot program.
<br></br>
I was hired to design the entire participant portal, which [The Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/magazine/2022/10/24/universal-basic-income/) said was "orders of magnitude more attractive and 
intuitive than any government website in history."
