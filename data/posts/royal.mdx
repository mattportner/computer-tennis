# Royal
[royal.io](https://royal.io)

<br></br>

Royal is a digital platform that sells music rights in the form of NFTs.
<br></br>
I briefly worked with Royal in the months surrounding its launch. I designed a variety of flows for the product, including artist profile management and fan voting.
